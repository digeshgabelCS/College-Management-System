About Uttam Memorial College


Uttam Memorial College is a premier Institute, established in 2011. It is located in Raigarh, Chhattisgarh. Institute offers 6 courses which are Degree and Diploma in Full Time mode. Overall, Institute offers 5 UG courses. Institute enjoys a good reputation for courses like PG Diploma, BCA, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc., UG Diploma in the streams of IT & Software, Accounting & Commerce, Business & Management Studies and Science. Students can opt courses from these. Over the period, Uttam Memorial College has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of IT & Software, Accounting & Commerce, Business & Management Studies and Science. Institute has a capacity of taking 680 students overall under all courses. Institute has a well supported campus with facilities like Computer Labs, Science Lab, Library, Sports, Wi-Fi Campus. Institute has 1 well equipped library with collection of 3612 books.

Uttam Memorial College is recognized by Chhattisgarh Government and currently affiliated to Shaheed Nandkumar Patel Vishwavidyalaya, Raigarh, Chhattisgarh. The courses approved by Shaheed Nandkumar Patel Vishwavidyalaya, Raigarh to impart education to student are BCA, DCA, BBA, B.COM, M.COM, B.SC, M.SC, BA, PGDCA, UG Diploma these courses provide the wide gamut to the student to opt scientific, commercial and professional courses in further studies adding to that the graduate also become competent for the employment in specific areas. On the basis of our expertise in education activities Chhattisgarh Government recognized us as vocation training provider thus it opens the door for drop out student from schooling and as well as pass out higher secondary etc. to become employable by the skill development various training which make them economically independent and serve the society well.

We believe that dreams can be realize through good governance, quality, teaching, personality development through extra curriculum activity within and without the campus.
